About RoadWayz

Calm, Confident & Professional Tuition

Hi, I'm Sandeep Bir Kaur. I am a local, DVSA Approved Driving Instructor based in Fife covering Kirkcaldy, Glenrothes, Leven and other surrounding areas.

With over 24 years of driving experience, I founded RoadWayz to provide a safe, supportive environment where students can learn at their own pace. My teaching philosophy is built on patience and connection. I believe that when a student feels calm, they learn faster and retain more.

I pride myself on being reliable and punctual. Every lesson is planned in advance to ensure you make consistent progress towards your test, getting the full value out of every hour you spend in the car.

Multilingual

Undivided Attention

A pressure-free environment with my full focus. Learn at your own pace.
